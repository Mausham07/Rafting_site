Whitewater rafting is offered by Shield Rafting. With more than ten years of expertise, we consistently raise the bar for the greatest rafting adventures on the Snake River and Salmon River. Simply said, we are the finest in terms of comprehensive service, top-notch gear, and knowledgeable river guides.
